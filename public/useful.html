    • THE EXAM ITSELF

    7.5 hours long | 4 Categories (scores from 118 - 132) | Total Score ranges from 472 - 528
    4 categories:

    1. Biological and biochemical foundations of living systems
    2. Chemical and physical foundations of biological systems
    3. Psychological, social, and biological foundations of behavior
    4. Critical analysis and reasoning skills (CARS)


    CONTENT REVIEW

    Kaplan and Princeton Review are considered best content review books. Kaplan is more high yield while Princeton Review is more dense but will cover every last inch of what you need to know (also less pictures).
    Khan Academy has MCAT prep videos. The AAMC paid Khan Academy to make the only official prep videos so it has access to all the AAMC material. Only accesible until 2026
    MileDownMD Review Sheets - really good summaries.
    Miledown Anki Decks
    For Psychology and Sociology read through 300 pg doc from Khan Academy.


    PRACTICE QUESTIONS

    Official AAMC practice questions!
    UWorld question bank has thousands of unofficial practice questions. Solid questions with explanations for each answer.

    PRACTICE EXAMS

    The best practice exams are the ones released by the AAMC.
    The common practice test makers are Kaplan, Blueprint, Altius, and Princeton Review which are all usually harder than the AAMC tests themselves.
    Breakdown of difficulty of practice tests versus the real thing (easiest --> hardest):

  • Kaplan : add 2-3 pts to your score
  • Blueprint : add 3-4 pts to your score
  • Altius : add 5-6 pts to your score
  • Princeton Review : hard.
